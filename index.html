Linus Torvalds

The creator of the Linux kernel

Timeline of Linus Torvalds:

  • 1969 - Born on the 28th of December in Helsinki, Finland
  • 1988 - Attended University of Helsinki
  • 1989 - Joined the Finnish Navy Nyland Brigade (due to the military conscription in Finland)
  • 1990 - Continued studing at university, this was when Linus was first exposed to Unix
  • 1991 - Begain his work on Linux
  • 1994 - Released version 1.0 of Linux
  • 1996 - Graduated with a master's degree in computer science
  • 2010 - Became a U.S. citizen
  • Present - Linus continues to work on maintaining the Linux kernel

Check out his Wikipedia page for more information.
